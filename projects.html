CALYNXX: THE LAST RIDE

Genre: Adventure / Exploration • Mobile/PC Concept Book

After a brutal medieval war, a legendary knight dies weeks after victory. His loyal war horse, Calynxx, does not understand death. Believing its master is alive somewhere in the kingdom, Calynxx begins a journey to find him.

Created as a complete mobile game concept for a skills-evaluation assignment. Covers core game idea & mechanics, player goals & progression, difficulty curve, and 5–10 level ideas or flow.
