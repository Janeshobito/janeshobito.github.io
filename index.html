NARRATIVE PHILOSOPHY

Author’s Statement

My name is Janeshwaran. I am a narrative-driven creator shaped by lived emotional contrast—family conflict, happiness, sadness, isolation, and connection. My interest in narrative design grew from observing how people react, hide reactions, and survive emotionally.

The dissonance between what is felt and what is shown became foundational to how I approach characters, psychology, and story.

Personal Lens

I exist between loneliness and solitude. I enjoy being social, yet resist it. My stories grow from emotional ambiguity, internal conflict, and contradiction.

Design Ethos

  • Choice defines character
  • Emotion drives mechanics
  • Every decision has a cost

My goal is not to create perfect stories — but honest ones that endure.

I am Janeshwaran. This is not a conclusion — but a structure.
